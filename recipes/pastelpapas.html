Pastel De Papas

one of my favorites dishes

"pastel de papas"(potato pie) is a dish consisting of a layer of potatoes on top of a layer of seasoned minced meat, with or without a potato base underneath. It is consumed mainly in South American countries.

ingredients

  • 1 k of potatos
  • Â½ k of minced meat
  • 1 onion
  • 1/2 bell peper
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 stock cube
  • garlic powder
  • paprika
  • 25 g of butter
  • milk
  • nutmeg
  • oil
  • salt
  • pepper

steps

  1. slice the potatos in cubes and put them to boil whit salt.
  2. chop the onion, garlic and bell peper.
  3. heat the oil in a pot or pan and fry the onion, peper and garlic.
  4. When the onion is transparent, add the meat and fry it while it falls apart with a spoon.
  5. Season, add the bouillon cube, the garlic powder, the paprika and cook the meat for about 15 minutes.
  6. Once the potatoes are boiled, make a hot puree with the butter and milk. Season.
  7. Put a base of puree in a baking dish, add the rest of the filling (meat) on top, let it cool down a bit and place another layer of puree. To distribute it, dip the spoon in cold water.
  8. Bake in a hot oven for 15 to 20 minutes, or until the top is crispy.
  9. You can add minced eggs, chopped green olives, or raisins to the meat filling.
